Features for Sellers

Create and Manage Product Listings

Sellers can list and manage their products, including setting prices, adding descriptions, and uploading images.

  • Add new products with detailed descriptions.
  • Set prices and manage inventory.
  • Upload high-quality images to showcase products.
  • Edit or remove product listings as needed.

Order Management

Track and manage incoming orders, accept or reject them, and monitor order status.

  • View and manage all incoming orders.
  • Accept or reject orders based on availability.
  • Monitor the status of orders from placement to delivery.
  • Communicate with buyers regarding order details.

Sales Analytics

Gain insights into sales performance, top-selling products, and revenue trends to optimize business strategies.

  • Track sales performance over time.
  • Identify top-selling products and categories.
  • Analyze revenue trends and customer behavior.
  • Make data-driven decisions to improve sales strategies.

Payment Management

Secure payment handling for sellers, enabling them to track earnings and manage withdrawals.

  • Track all earnings from product sales.
  • Manage withdrawals and payments securely.
  • View detailed transaction history.
  • Ensure secure and timely payment processing.
